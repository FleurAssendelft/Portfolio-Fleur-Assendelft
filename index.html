    Hi i'm Fleur

    My name is Fleur Assendelft. I am born and raised in Hillegom, a small town in Zuid-Holland. I live here with my parents, brother and cat. Every saturday I work in a cheeseshop in Noordwijkerhout.

    I am a spontaneous, creative, curious and a real teamplayer. I love cooking, baking, photography and I adore animals. In my free time I like to spend time with my friends and family.

    Currently, I am in my second year of Cummunication and Multimedia Design. I get educated to become an Interaction Designer. During this journy I created a passion for coding and designing. I love to create a design that the client loves and that works on all devices.
